# Welcome to my first blog post!

Hello! My name is Nicholas Dobos, and I'm a photographer based in Newnan, GA. 

My full-time job is web development/software engineering though, so I decided it would be good to leverage that skillset to make this blog and boost my online presence regarding my favorite _past time_: photography!

One piece of wisdom that I've heard before is that we should all spend a little time each day writing.

In an effort to implement that habit, I've decided to create this blog. 

In this blog I will talk about anything that I want, but mostly photography.

That's all for now, I'm going to leave some images below for people to get an idea of what I like to photograph.
